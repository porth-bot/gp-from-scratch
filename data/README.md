# Data

`co2_mm_mlo.txt` — monthly mean atmospheric CO2 at Mauna Loa Observatory,
Hawaii (March 1958 – present), in ppm. Retrieved 2026-07-03 from
https://gml.noaa.gov/ccgg/trends/data.html
(direct file: https://gml.noaa.gov/webdata/ccgg/trends/co2/co2_mm_mlo.txt).

Credit, as requested by the providers: Dr. Xin Lan, NOAA/GML
(gml.noaa.gov/ccgg/trends/) and Dr. Ralph Keeling, Scripps Institution of
Oceanography (scrippsco2.ucsd.edu/). U.S. government data, public domain.

Columns used: decimal date (col 3) and monthly average CO2 in ppm (col 4);
rows are whitespace-delimited, comments start with `#`.
